Reflections

Meta is a big company that functions at an incredibly fast pace. It's easy to get washed up in a plethora of technical docs and 50+ cross-functional partners (not kidding...).

To swim, and not drown... swim with peers you trust, make sure one-pagers are actually one page from your POV, high-fidelity prototypes follow a clear linear flow, review decks are inspiring and succinct, and decisions are anchored by data and design principles.

In essence, keep it simple. But at Meta, it's inevitable you get lost in the sauce once in a while.
