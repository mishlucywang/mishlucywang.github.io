Fruto

  • Industrial & Product Designer
  • Feb 2025
  • Cupertino, CA

Wouldn't it be so cute if kids were running around in glossy cherry helmets with a green stem bobbing around as they play in the park? Perhaps this helmet could be a fun creative outlet for Michelle... maybe she's just trying to heal her inner child... maybe it's a good idea...

Sitting at a park one night, she saw...

a little girl zipping around on her scooter. With the bright floodlights from the basketball court glinting off her glossy helmet, it almost looked like a big juicy lollipop had been fastened atop her head.

As she rolled toward the park's exit, her little feet kicking with all its might, Michelle's friend slouched beside her and sighed, "Kids are so cute... maybe I actually do want kids."

Indeed, that little girl was impossibly cute.

Michelle watched and murmured, "Yeah... maybe kids aren't that bad. I just wish she had a stem coming out of her helmet, like a cherry stem."

Then, Michelle and her friend instinctively looked at each other. In that fleeting moment, they both knew exactly what would've made her even cuter. And on that one autumn night at the park, as they watched her scooter into the distance, the last glimmer of light bouncing off her helmet—something small, something whimsical, something that made the night feel just a little more magical.
