Duodaily

  • Product Design Lead
  • Oct 2021, 2 week sprint
  • Remote

Duodaily is an immersive way to learn... daily. By integrating with everyday apps like Google Calendar. Learn that your typical 8:00am "Wake up & Coffee" is "Wake up & 咖啡" in Chinese.

Learning a new language is very difficult and time consuming...

No matter how "fun" and "engaging" existing solutions are, it's hard to stay consistent when learning a new language requires so much commitment.

Learning languages should feel natural

Almost like you're immersed in a culture, learning vocabulary relevant to your daily life.

Sign up & add apps

Learn relevant words

Immersion through messages

Review & retain

Track streaks & rewards on profile

How it works

Duodaily connects with other apps and relies heavily on API integration to gradually immerse users into a new language.

Users can select which apps they want to learn with, then Duodaily begins highlighting key words or phrases to make sure users are learning relevant vocabulary words in their desired language.

Personalized learning and seamless integration

Learn bits and pieces of a new language as you check your schedule on Google Calendar.

Instead of doom scrolling Twitter, stay updated on the latest Tweet with Duodaily to continue learning bits and pieces of a new language as you go.

Toggle and switch between your top apps

Try it out

Feel free to go through the clickable prototype here.

Project Takeaways

Instead of focusing on what's missing in the market, target what people are actually missing in their lives.

Also, understanding the ecosystem of digital products users already have allows you to be resourceful. By leveraging existing technologies, you can build seamless experiences that can naturally integrate into everyday life.

The unique approach and proposal of Duodaily granted Michelle a Design Fellowship at Kleiner Perkins and an opportunity at Duolingo.
